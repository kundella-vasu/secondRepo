Bullet 350

Royal Enfield Bullet 350 is powered by 346 cc engine. This Bullet 350 engine generates a power of 19.36 PS @ 5250 rpm and a torque of 28 Nm @ 4000 rpm. Royal Enfield Bullet 350 gets Drum brakes in the front and rear. The kerb weight of Bullet 350 is 191 Kg.

Royal Enfield could launch a more affordable variant of the Thunderbird 350 soon.
